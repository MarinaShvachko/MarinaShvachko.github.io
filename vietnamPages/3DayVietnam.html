It's unfair that in vacation I have to wake up so early.

The breakfast time is from 6 am to 10 am at our hotel, so every day we have to wake up with healp of alarm clock. Think next time we should try to go abroad without breakfast at a hotel. The best option to rent an apart of course.

The breakfasts were good. Some times I tried something special, like crocodile or Kimchi.

After dinner we decided to go to pagoda Long Sơn. The distance isn't long, only about 3 km frome our hotel, but a lot of mopeds and absence of organised traffic made our trip much longer. Usually, people get there by bus.
